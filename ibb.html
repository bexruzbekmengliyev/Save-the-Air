Main Causes of Air Pollution

Vehicle Emissions

Cars, trucks, and buses release NOx, CO, and particulate matter daily.

Factory Smoke

Industrial plants emit sulfur dioxide, heavy metals, and toxic chemicals.

Plastic Burning

Open burning releases dioxins and microplastics into the atmosphere.

Deforestation

Fewer trees mean less CO2 absorption and disrupted oxygen cycles.

Construction Dust

PM2.5 and PM10 from sites severely impact respiratory health.

Fossil Fuels

Coal, oil, and gas power plants are the largest source of global CO2.

Know the Harmful Gases

CO₂ (Carbon Dioxide)

Primary greenhouse gas. Traps heat, causing global warming and ocean acidification.

CH₄ (Methane)

80x more potent than CO₂ over 20 years. Released from livestock, landfills, and drilling.

Smoke & PM2.5

Tiny particles penetrate lungs and bloodstream, causing asthma, heart disease, and cancer.

How to Reduce Air Pollution

Commute Smart

Use public transport, bike, or walk. Carpool when possible to cut emissions.

Save Energy

Switch to LED bulbs, unplug devices, and use smart thermostats at home.

Plant & Protect

Trees absorb 22kg of CO₂ yearly. Support urban greening projects.

Reduce Waste

Recycle, compost, and avoid single-use plastics to lower landfill methane.

Clean Energy Solutions

Solar Power

Harness sunlight for electricity. Zero emissions during operation. Costs dropped 90% in a decade.

Wind Energy

Turbines convert kinetic wind energy into clean power. Ideal for coastal and open plains.

Act Locally, Impact Globally

Tree Planting Campaign

Join community drives. One mature tree produces enough oxygen for 2 people daily. We've planted 12,400+ trees this year.

Smart Recycling

Sort waste at source. Glass, paper, metal, and plastic can be reused endlessly. Reduces landfill methane by up to 60%.
